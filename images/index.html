WENK ENDRINGS

Wenk is specialized and world leader in manufacturing aluminium endrings for rotary screens in various repeats: from 518 mm to 1200 mm, for Stork, Reggiani, J.Zimmer, P.Zimmer, Buser and Meccanotessile printing machines. Wenk production is 100% made in Italy, from aluminium alloy to the finished product. Our endrings are sold worldwide directly by WENK to customers or through our agents and distributors.

Our production is based on the use of high quality aluminium alloy and a turning carried out by CNC Tooding machines. Each endring is then checked with advanced controls in order to ensure perfect use. This painstaking manufacturing enables us to supply our customers with flawless product.

WENK SCREENS

We can supply all kind of nickel screens. Repeat from 640mm to 1018mm. Lenght from 1410 up to 3500mm and mesh from 40 to 195.

We also can supply every kind of special screen.

STRIPPING MACHINE

Our high pressure water stripping system is a completely engineered and made in Italy machine available in 2 different lenghts for all repeats from 640 to 1018. With our machine you can easily switch between repeats without manual intervention. Just insert the nickel screen with the new repeat and press "Start"

Very easy to use. Environmental friendly and harmless for operators.

Our machine also have a DRS (Dental removing system) with which you can easily remove dents. The screens stripped with our machine are as good as new.
